Additional Versions of Persephone's Story

Although Persephone's story is fairly consistent across most versions, there are still some variations between each.

Main Story Variation
Initially, Persephone was not happy about being kidnapped, but she came to love Hades. Persephone always wanted to leave but felt she had no choice but to stay.
Persephone didn't know eating the pomegranate would force her to partially stay in the Underworld. Persephone chooses to fall for eating the pomegranates.
Persephone ate 1 pomegranate. Persephone ate three, four, six, or seven seeds (according to various versions).
Persephone chose to eat the seeds, with no suspicion. Hades forced her to eat the seeds.
Persephone was kidnapped. Persephone wasn't actually kidnapped, but instead she willingly chose to go to the Underworld.
